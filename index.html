Our Cheeses

We handmake original and unique vegan cheeses in a variety of delicious flavors that appeal to everyone. The rich, bold, and smooth texture of our cashew cheeses are like no other on the market. Our Caramelized Onion Camembert was recently recognized as one of the top 3 individual vegan cheeses in the United States by VegNews Magazine! We use organic, fair-trade cashews and hand-craft our cheeses locally, carefully sourcing and choosing ingredients for maximum benefit to health and the environment.

A little about us...

"We have been making organic, vegan cashew cheese for nearly five years and we love seeing people’s positive reactions when they taste our cheeses. Many customers cannot believe that that our products are vegan because the taste is so similar to dairy. Our most recent accolade was #1 feature in the VegNews article, “2018 is the Year of the Vegan Wine and Cheese Pairings."
